About Parakration

In Olden times, the Greeks participated in Pankration - a no-holds-barred event where everything went and next-to-nothing was beyond limits. Centuries since, science has continued to follow a much similar modus operandi, with the turn of every century bringing with it new innovations and ideas that continually redefined the way man saw his world. It is in this very spirit that Spectrum, LGS Paragon’s Science Society, invites you to attend ParaKration IV- the Fourth Edition of the annual full-scale Science Olympiad of its kind at the college level. Here at ParaKration, we seek to the ubiquity of science and its applications in modern society. Events have been designed specifically keeping this purpose in mind: they require not only the knowledge of science but proficiency in a variety of fields, many of which are completely disparate from the other, such as Media, Art, Gaming and so on.
